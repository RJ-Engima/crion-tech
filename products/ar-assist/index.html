A Comprehensive Solution: Clonos AR Assist

Clonos AR Assist offers a smart, augmented reality-powered solution for frontline operators, providing:

Augmented Reality Guidance

Real-time, step-by-step instructions overlaid onto the physical environment for precise maintenance execution.

QR Code Integration

Instant access to detailed asset information and instructions by scanning QR codes on assets.

Comprehensive Asset Information

Access extensive asset data, including specifications and maintenance history, through AR overlays.

Data Logging

Seamlessly capture and log maintenance activities directly within the AR interface for accurate record-keeping.

Interactive Troubleshooting Tools

Efficiently resolve asset issues with AR-enhanced troubleshooting tools.
