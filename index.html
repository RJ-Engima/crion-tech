What is CLONOS ?

CLONOS is an advanced SaaS platform designed for maintenance and asset management. It leverages digital technologies like IoT, AR, and predictive analytics to streamline operations, enhance asset reliability, and reduce downtime. By centralizing asset data, automating workflows, and providing real-time monitoring, CLONOS helps organizations improve efficiency, lower maintenance costs, and maintain regulatory compliance.

Want to reduce your Mean Time to Repair?

Our solution leverages predictive maintenance and real-time diagnostics to minimize downtime and streamline repair processes, ensuring your equipment is up and running quickly.
