What is CLONOS ?

CLONOS is an advanced SaaS platform designed for maintenance and asset management. It leverages digital technologies like IoT, AR, and predictive analytics to streamline operations, enhance asset reliability, and reduce downtime.

By centralizing asset data, automating workflows, and providing real-time monitoring, CLONOS helps organizations improve efficiency, lower maintenance costs, and maintain regulatory compliance.

Our Solutions

Condition Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of asset health to detect anomalies early and prevent failures. Utilize real-time data and predictive analytics to extend asset lifespan and reduce downtime.

CAR-Enabled Manuals

Augmented reality overlays for interactive step-by-step guides and troubleshooting. Enhance operator efficiency and accuracy by providing visual instructions directly on the equipment.

Digital Twins

Create digital replicas of physical assets for real-time monitoring and simulation. Optimize performance and predict maintenance needs through virtual modeling and scenario analysis.

Predictive Maintenance

Leverage IoT sensors and advanced analytics to anticipate maintenance needs. Reduce unplanned downtime and maintenance costs by addressing issues before they escalate.

Remote Asset Management

Monitor and manage assets remotely with integrated IoT and cloud technologies. Ensure continuous operations and quick response times, regardless of location.

Want to reduce your Mean Time to Repair?

Our solution leverages predictive maintenance and real-time diagnostics to minimize downtime and streamline repair processes, ensuring your equipment is up and running quickly.
